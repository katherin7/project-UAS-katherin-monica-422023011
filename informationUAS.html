TWICE WORLD TOUR

K-Pop Phenoms TWICE Return To The Global Stage For 5th World Tour ‘Ready To Be’ | Live in Germany in September 2023 Today, international K-Pop sensations TWICE announced the European dates of their 5TH WORLD TOUR ‘READY TO BE’. The worldwide run will hit several cities across Korea, Australia, Japan, Canada, the U.S., UK and Europe. This outing marks the first time ever for a female K-Pop group to perform at Berlin's Mercedes-Benz Arena. Due to the high demand during the Ticketmaster Presale the band announced an additional Berlin show on 13 September. Tickets go on sale this Friday (28 April) at 10 am CET here at Ticketmaster Germany.

    TWICE 5TH WORLD TOUR ‘READY TO BE’ Dateng ke Jakarta

    JYP Entertainment girl group, TWICE, akhirnya ngumumin kalau mereka bakalan masukin Jakarta sebagai bagian rangkaian tur TWICE 5TH WORLD TOUR ‘READY TO BE’ yang pastinya langsung disambut para fans mereka di Tanah Air.

    Popularitas TWICE nggak bisa dipungkiri kalau emang gede banget di Indonesia, bahkan juga menyentuh para cowok yang pengen nonton Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, dan Tzuyu secara langsung. Itulah kenapa TWICE mau memilih Jakarta untuk tur terbaru mereka ini.

    TWICE 5TH WORLD TOUR ‘READY TO BE’ Dateng ke Jakarta

    Rencananya TWICE 5TH WORLD TOUR ‘READY TO BE’ bakalan diadakan di Jakarta International Stadium pada 23 Desember 2023. Selain tampil di Jakarta, TWICE juga bakalan konser di Melbourne, Mexico City, dan Sao Paulo. Konsep world tour yang beneran dibawain oleh TWICE udah pasti siap disambut antusiasme dalam proses penjualan tiket.

    Tunggu update tanggal penjualan dan harga tiket dalam waktu dekat.
